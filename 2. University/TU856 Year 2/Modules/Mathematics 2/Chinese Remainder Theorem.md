## CRT Formula
To find X from equations,
$X=r_{1}(mod\>n_{1})$
$X=r_{2}(mod\>n_{2})$
$X=r_{2}(mod\>n_{2})$

Use this formula,
$X=m_{1}r_{1}s_{1}+m_{2}r_{2}s_{2}+m_{3}r_{3}s_{3}$

Where
$M=n_{1}*n_{2}*n_{3}$

$m_{1}=\frac{M}{r_{1}}\>,\>m_{2}=\frac{M}{r_{2}}\>,\>m_{3}=\frac{M}{r_{3}}$

$m_{1}s_{1}≡1mod\>n_{1}$ (Inverse of $m_{1},m_{2},m_{3}$)
