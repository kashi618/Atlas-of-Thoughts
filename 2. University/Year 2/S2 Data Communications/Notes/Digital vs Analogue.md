## Characteristics and Examples
### Digital
**Characteristics**
- Discrete pulses
- Limited by 1 or 0
- Square waves

**Examples**
- Digital watches
- CDs, DVDs, Blu-rays
- MP3/MP4 files

### Analogue
**Characteristics**
- Continuous wave
- Can be any value
- Sine waves

**Examples**
- Traditional wall clock
- Heart rate monitor
- Vinyl records
