## What is it?
The process of sending digital/analogue data between a transmitter and a receiver.

Data is encoded onto a [[Transmission Signal|transmission signal]], which is then transmitted across a transmission system

Encoding is changing the characteristic of a signal to, to represent the data. The more changes made to a signal, the more data can be transmitted.
