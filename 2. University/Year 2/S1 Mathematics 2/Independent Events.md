## What is it?
Two events $A$ and $B$ are considered independent if the occurrence of one, does NOT affect the other.

## Checking if Events are Independent
**If two events $A$ and $B$ ARE independent**
$P(A)*P(B)=(A∩B)$

**If two events $A$ and $B$ are NOT independent**
$P(A)*P(B)\neq(A∩B)$
