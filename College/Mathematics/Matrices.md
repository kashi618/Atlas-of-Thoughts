## What is a Matrix?
An array of numbers in a particular order
## Order of a Matrix
 R x C
 Start with the row, then the column. (RC Car)
 
$A\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4\\
5 & 6
\end{bmatrix}$  Three Rows x Two Columns  

$A_{12} = 2$
$A_{31} = 5$

## Square Matrices
