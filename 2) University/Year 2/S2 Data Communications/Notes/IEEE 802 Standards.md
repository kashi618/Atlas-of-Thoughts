## IEEE 802
This is a family of IEEE standards dealing with LANs and MANs.

### IEEE 802.3 - Ethernet
- Defines wired ethernet ([[CSMA and CSMA CD|CSMA/CD]])
- Most common LAN technology
- Physical layer, and MAC layer specifications

### IEEE 802.11 - Wireless LAN 
Also know as **WiFi**
- Defines wireless networking standards
- Includes 802.11a/b/g/n/ac/ax
- Operates in 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands

### IEEE 802.5 - Token Ring
- Defines the [[LAN Topologies|token ring topology]]
