## Issues
LANs are often local, because issues can pop up:
- Attenuation (Signal degredation due to length)
- Contention (Multiple devices using a single medium, causing slowdown)
- Latency

To fix these issues, we have three devices:
- Repeaters
- [[Bridges]]
- Routers
