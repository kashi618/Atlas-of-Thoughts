## Repeaters
Repeaters are used to overcome the distance limitation of signals. ([[Attenuation|attenuation]]).
- For example, a 10Base5 LAN has a length restriction of around 500m

**Repeater Implementation**
![[LAN Repeater.png]]

### Repeater Functions
- A repeater only connects segments of a LAN. It does not connect two LANs togethor.
- A repeater regenerates attenuated bits. For each bit received, a fresh bit is sent back out.
- A repeater forwards every bit/frame without filtering, or modification of any kind.
