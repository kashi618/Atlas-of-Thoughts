## LAN Topologies
LAN topologies are the physical or logical arrangement of devices and the connections between them in a LAN. It defines how they are interconnected, and how data flows between them

There are three LAN topologies to consider:
- [[Bus LAN]]
- [[Ring LAN]]
- [[Star LAN]]
