## Greedy Algorithms
A step by step approach to solving a problem, that focuses on finding the most optimal local choice at each step, resulting in finding a global optimal solution. Greed algorithms
- Chooses the best immediate option. (doesn't look at past or future choices)


**Examples**
- [[Kruskal's Algorithm]]
- [[Prim's Algorithm]]
