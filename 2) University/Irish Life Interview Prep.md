### Team Work and What I Learned
**Game Off 2024**
Situation
- I worked in a team of 4 for the "Game Off 2024" game jam

Task
- Our goal was to be able to build a complete game from scratch, and deliver it before a strict deadline of 30 days. And hopefully achieving a high 

Action
- During the first day, I stepped in to organize the group. I am close friends with each of the members, so I delegated tasks to each member, based on their strengths. One member was tasked with designing the looks, appearance, and the interface, another member was the background music and sound effects, whilst the last member and I focuses on developing the programming and logic of the game. 

Result
- We were able to deliver the game on time, and we placed 115 out of 509 entries. 
- From this, I learned how to assign tasks based on each of my teammates strengths, which is a skill I want to bring to this summer placement
